### Listing tips for £1000 tier
- Add 4–6 screenshots: staff panel GUI, tag GUI, punishment screen, rank ladder, chat format,
  disguise menu. Buyers at this price expect to *see* the product.
- Record a 60–90s demo video of staff mode, a punishment, and the tag/disguise GUIs.
- State clearly what the buyer receives: jar only, or jar + source; and your support/update terms.
- Pin the requirements (1.8.8, Java 8) near the top to prevent refund requests.
- Mention the included documentation — it justifies a premium price and reduces support load.
